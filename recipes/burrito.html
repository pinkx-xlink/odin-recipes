Knock Off Taco Bell

Description

I am one of those disgusting horrible people who would eat Taco Bell 5 nights a week if I could. However, I want to live past 27, so here is my personal knock off T-Bell recipe for when I want to satisfy my cravings and keep some of my dignity as well.

Before we get into it, I'd like to let you know: THIS IS SUPER EASY AND CHEAP! If I, a 22-year-old who works fulltime on 4 hours of sleep per night just to barely be able to pay for my studio apartment, can make this, then you should have no trouble.

The thing that makes this recipe so reliable, easy, and versatile is that most of the ingredients can be switched out or completely forgotten without compromising the meal. For example, I use chicken because I almost always have some on hand, but you could use ground beef or steak instead if you have a healthier budget than I do.

Ingredients

  • tortillas (I usually have flour on hand)
  • chicken
  • shredded cheese
  • beans
  • Spanish rice
  • tomato paste
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • cumin
  • salt
  • ground pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • ground oregano
  • oil

Steps

  1. Put tomato paste and thawed chicken in your slow cooker; cook on high for 1 1/2 - 2 hrs.
  2. After chicken is cooked, shred it with two forks. Add back into slow cooker and mix in seasonings.
  3. Warm tortillas so they don't break when you roll them.
  4. Add shredded cheese, chicken, rice, beans, and veggies and sauces of your choice to tortillas.
  5. Roll the burrito closed.
  6. Add oil to pan over medium-high heat.
  7. Add burrito to pan WITH THE OPENING SIDE FACE-DOWN - this helps prevent all the goodies in the burrito from spilling out. Let sear for a minute or two (or until golden).
  8. Once the opening side is as gold (or burnt) as you like, flip the buritto and sear the other side as well.
