Whatever's-In-The-Fridge Omelette

Description

This one is pretty self-explanatory. Have you got eggs and some veggies or meat that you need to use up? If so, keep reading so we can clear out some fridge space AND put a hearty meal on the table within 15 minutes.

This omelette cooks up quickly with as many or as few extra bells and whistles as you choose. I like to throw mushrooms, spinach, and diced tomatoes on mine, but you might want to add ham, green peppers, onions, or maybe nothing at all to yours.

This can be a meal all on its own, however it also pairs well with has browns, sausages, bacon, toast, or some fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • shredded cheese
  • milk
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • cayenne pepper

    • Optional:

  • spinach
  • mushrooms
  • diced tomatoes

Steps

  1. In a bowl, crack 2-3 eggs. Mix with 3/4 cup milk.
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan. Let cook for a few minutes until bottom starts turning gold.
  4. Sprinkle salt, ground pepper, and spices of your choice on the omelette.
  5. Sprinkle a thin layer of cheese.
  6. Add veggies and meat, then another layer of cheese.
  7. Once the cheese has started to melt, fold the edges of the omelette over the center to close.
  8. Serve your beautiful Frankenstein's Breakfast on your finest china!
