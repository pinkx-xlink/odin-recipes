Poor Man's Ramen, Without the Malnutrition

Description

As you might have noticed from the previous two recipes, I am a fan of versatile and easy meals. This ramen recipe is no exception!

Perhaps I'm biased since I'm 22 years old and a victim of capitalism (I am poor lol). But perhaps you're also a broke 20-something-year-old who foams at the mouth over cheap chemical-packed meals. If this sounds like you, stay tuned!

Ingredients

  • water
  • ramen noodles (I like the Neoguri band seafood flavor)
  • your choice of veggies/meat

    • Optional:

  • eggs
  • chopped green onion
  • sliced pork

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add your noodles and seasoning packet to the water.
  3. Let cook for 3-5 minutes. If you don't wish to add anything else, you're done! The noodles will be cool enough to eat in a few minutes. :)
  4. I let my noodles cook for about a minute to soften before I crack 2 eggs directly into the pot. I lower the heat to LOW and put a lid on the top.
  5. Let the ramen cook on LOW for about 3 more minutes. DON'T let it cook too long, or else the egg yolks will become solid.
  6. Poor the ramen into a large bowl.
  7. I like to add sliced pork and chopped green onions, but you might like to add chicken, beef, peppers, mushrooms, shrimp, etc. Go crazy! :P
  8. Serve your soup, king UwU
